# PolyLoop Personalized Role Dashboards

This prototype separates the platform into two purpose-built dashboard experiences.

## 1. Dustbin Owner Dashboard

Primary question: **What should I do with the plastic currently in my bins?**

Information order:
1. Urgent readiness or pickup action
2. Total plastic, ready quantity, earnings and device health
3. Live plastic inventory by smart bin
4. Collector offers and pickup decisions
5. Earnings and payment visibility
6. Device and sensor health
7. Positive environmental and community impact

Owner navigation:
- Overview
- My Smart Bins
- Plastic Inventory
- Pickup Requests
- Earnings
- Impact & Community
- Messages
- Point Settings

## 2. Plastic Collector Dashboard

Primary question: **Where is the best plastic supply and how should I collect it?**

Information order:
1. Highest-value supply and route opportunity
2. Matching supply, reserved quantity, route distance and margin
3. Supply map
4. Recommended lots matching demand
5. Suggested collection route
6. Saved demand alerts
7. Upcoming pickups
8. Reliable collection-point partners

Collector navigation:
- Overview
- Find Plastic
- Saved Demand
- My Route
- Reservations & Pickups
- Purchases & Payments
- Supplier Partners
- Recycling Impact
- Messages

## Files

- `index.html` — role selection portal
- `owner-dashboard.html` — dustbin-owner dashboard
- `collector-dashboard.html` — plastic-collector dashboard
- `styles.css` — shared visual system
- `app.js` — interactions, modals, filters and notifications
- `owner-dashboard-standalone.html` — portable single-file owner prototype
- `collector-dashboard-standalone.html` — portable single-file collector prototype
